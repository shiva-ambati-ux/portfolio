2023
Samsung

Edit Suggestions - Samsung Gallery

The business objective for Samsung as a smartphone OEM is to increase native experience timeshare to establish differentiation against competition with exclusive features.

In the visual journey of capture, edit & share, research shows that many photos are taken during important moments, but only few are shareworthy. This is primarily due to time constraints, unable to correlate right parameters for desired output, unawareness of tools and features. This concept simplifies the journey & increases the share-worthiness of photos.

2023
Samsung

Enhanced Search

Smartphone users often struggle to find and use native features, leading to a reliance on third-party apps. The approach of this concept is to make features searchable within the native search, suggest related features based on search phrases, and provide quick actions from apps. This includes showing the most likely actions to be taken & navigations after a search. Further, enhancing capabilities with ability to search with synonyms and related words.

Samsung

Chakra - Camera Experience Research

Camera is a key buying factor for the majority of Indian smartphone users. Due to the high usage and impact of social media, especially among younger users with evolved needs.

Research Objective: Innovate camera and gallery features to position Samsung smartphones as leaders in the Indian market. Explore user capture/edit behaviors, decode what is a good photo/ video and assess differences across demographics and price tiers. Uncover insights into capture motivations and editing intentions.

Patient Connect

CareStack's Patient Connect Kiosk, a tool that facilitates patient self check-in and document submission at hospitals, was facing issues of low task completion rates. To address this, the design was significantly streamlined. Non-essential features were removed, and a contextual information architecture was implemented. A clear home screen with a hierarchy of tasks was created, and the document signing process was simplified for better usability.
