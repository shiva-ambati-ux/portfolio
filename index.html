01 / EDIT SUGGESTIONS - SAMSUNG GALLERY

The business objective for Samsung as a smartphone OEM is to increase native experience timeshare to establish differentiation against competition with exclusive features.

In the visual journey of capture, edit & share, research shows that many photos are taken during important moments, but only few are shareworthy. This is primarily due to time constraints, unable to correlate right parameters for desired output, unawareness of tools and features. This concept simplifies the journey & increases the share-worthiness of photos.

Qualitative Research, Usage Data Analysis, Competitive Benchmarking, UX Design, UI Design.

02 / ENHANCED SEARCH

Smartphone users often struggle to find and use native features, leading to a reliance on third-party apps.

The approach of this concept is to make features searchable within the native search, suggest related features based on search phrases, and provide quick actions from apps. This includes showing the most likely actions to be taken & navigations after a search. Further, enhancing capabilities with ability to search with synonyms and related words.

Qualitative Research, Usage Data Analysis, Competitive Benchmarking, UX Design.
